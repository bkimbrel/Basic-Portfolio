About Me

Hi. Bonnie and my friendly coding companion, Ichigo here. Life is an adventure and I love exploring new things. Curiosity is what has brought me to trying out web development. I love the idea that that you can always be creating, whether it is function or aesthetics.

Living in the beautiful state of Colorado, provides me the regular opportunity to explore new restaurants, venues, art, hiking trails, and an abundant music scene. I am a bit of a foodie so; I have a running list of new places and dishes to try. When I remember to, I try to blog and share my experiences. I hope to one day to write a book, highlighting some of my favorite things to do in Colorado.
